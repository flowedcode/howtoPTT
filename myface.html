*helpful tips*

When you can't find out how to get help when you need it, a great way to talk to them is seeing them after class. This is a descreet, easy, and understanding way to talk to them.

If you need help knowing what to say, bring a freind or a parent and set a meeting to talk to them. PS., this does not mean your helper does the whole thing for you. It is also helpful to write a note to yourself so you know what to say
